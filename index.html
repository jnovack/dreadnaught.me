Who is Dreadnaught?

Wade "Dreadnaught" Penfold is an on-stream analyst for Heroes of the Storm. He was a professional Support player and shotcaller for Tempo Storm (previously Symbiote Gaming) and spends his spare time streaming under the Tempo Storm name or coaching up-and-coming teams.
